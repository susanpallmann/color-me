No two experiences are quite the same color.

Mental health issues affect everyone differently, and this can make it hard for us to relate. This project aims to turn these experiences visual and start a conversation about the functions we take for granted in respect to mental health.

Build Your Experience

Why this project?

Each experience on this website is populated by the objects that populate our everyday lives. Despite the familiarity, however, those that live with mental health issues can attest that sometimes even the everyday can feel strange.

This project aims to draw attention to the fact that the world we interact with isn't always easy.

Another goal is sharing. Sharing an experience grows empathy and spreads awareness.

Finally, while everyone is different, nobody is alone, and you can view others' experiences here too.
